About Labdhi Engineering Pvt. Ltd.

Labdhi Engineering was established in 2003 to meet the demand for dependable mechanical components in the industrial and infrastructure sectors.

Our facility in Ahmedabad handles end-to-end processes including manufacturing, stock management, and dispatch. We serve OEMs, system integrators, EPC contractors, and plant-level operations across India and key export markets.

What We Offer

  • Industrial Valves (Gate, Ball, Globe, Check, Solenoid)
  • Hydraulic Tube Fittings
  • Pressure Gauges and Instruments
  • Hydraulic Cylinders and Assemblies
  • Custom-configured flow and control components

Markets Served

  • Heavy Engineering and Fabrication
  • Automotive and Ancillary Units
  • Hydraulic System Integrators
  • Industrial Maintenance Contractors
  • Infrastructure and Utility Projects

Operational Strength

  • Dedicated in-house inspection and verification
  • Repeat batch traceability and clear labeling
  • Dispatch planning based on urgency and volume
  • Export processes aligned with regional compliance
