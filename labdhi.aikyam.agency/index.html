About us Explore

Supplying Industry. Supporting Infrastructure.

Labdhi Engineering Pvt. Ltd. manufactures and supplies critical mechanical components for infrastructure, engineering, and plant-based industries. Established in 2003, our facility in Ahmedabad supports both standard stock and made-to-order production.

Product categories include industrial valves, solenoid valves, hydraulic fittings, pressure gauges, and cylinders. All units are made from high-tensile materials and inspected before dispatch.

Exports supported in South Africa, Uganda, and Dubai. All orders are processed with packaging compliance and timeline adherence.
